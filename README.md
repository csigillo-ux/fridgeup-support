## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I add a product?**
Tap the green "+" button at the bottom of the screen and enter the product details manually or scan the barcode.

**How do I upgrade to PRO?**
Tap the settings icon (top right) to access the PRO upgrade screen.

**What's included in PRO?**
- Multiple lists (Fridge, Freezer, Pantry)
- iCloud Backup & sync across devices
- iOS Home Screen Widget
- 5 exclusive themes
- 5 alternative app icons
